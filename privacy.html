Privacy

Privacy Policy.

Effective date: July 4, 2026. This page explains how WidBar, the WidBar website and the widget showcase handle personal information.

Personal information

WidBar does not collect, use or sell your personal information. The WidBar website is a static site and does not include account login, forms, payments or user profiles.

Microsoft services

WidBar and its widgets are distributed through Microsoft Store and may use Microsoft platform services. Microsoft may collect, monitor or analyze information according to its own privacy policies.

Widget showcase

The showcase loads a public widget catalog to display available widgets. The catalog contains public metadata such as widget name, description, category, publisher, icon URL and Microsoft Store product links.

Third-party widgets

Some widgets may be created and published by developers other than Andrea Del Bello. I am not responsible for widgets, services, data collection, behavior or content provided by third-party widget developers. Please review the Microsoft Store listing and privacy information for each widget you install.

External links and contact

Links to Microsoft Store, GitHub, Discord, email and LinkedIn open external services. If you contact WidBar, the information you choose to send is used to respond to your message or support request.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. You should check this page periodically for changes.
